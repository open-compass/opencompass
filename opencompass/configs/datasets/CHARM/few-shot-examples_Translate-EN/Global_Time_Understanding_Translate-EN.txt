Q: Choose the correct answer according to the question. Today is Christmas Eve 1937. What is the date 10 days ago? Options:
(A) December 14, 1988
(B)December 14, 2026
(C)December 14, 1950
(D)July 14, 1938

A: Let's think step by step.
If today is Christmas Eve, 1937, then today's date is December 24, 1937. The 10 days before today is December 14, 1937, which is 12/14/1937. so the answer is (E).
